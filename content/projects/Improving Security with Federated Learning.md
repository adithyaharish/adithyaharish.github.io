To effectively preserve statistics from leakage, this study analyzes a novel framework using differential privacy (DP), in which synthetic noises are provided to parameters on the customers' side prior to aggregation, FLAG noise (FL with noise aggregated).The system analyses the dataset consisting of information about the client. Federated learning with Advanced Encryption Standard (AES) algorithm and Differential privacy is then applied. It is found that the Federated learning model have better privacy than the Differential privacy model and gives the accuracy of 97.3%.
