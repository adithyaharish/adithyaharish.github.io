This paper provides a hybrid methodology that incorporates both ARIMA and ANN models to take advantage of the unique characteristics of ARIMA and ANN models in linear and nonlinear modelling. The Hybrid model showed better accuracy and a root mean square error of 21,267 which is the lowest among other models compared.
